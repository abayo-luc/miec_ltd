Services

We are committed to providing the best possible construction expertise & service to ensure cost effective and successful projects.

Architectural and Structural Design of Buildings

MIEC Ltd does Architectural and structural design of both high-rise and low-rise residential buildings, commercial buildings and also institutional buildings as well as their detailed ground investigation.

Construction and Civil Works

MIEC Ltd does execute large construction and structural projects, namely buildings, roads, bridges and dams; and integrated maintenance and refurbishment works of construction facilities.

Construction Supervision and Consultancy

We help running a construction site by coordinating people, materials and equipment, inspecting the construction process, field testing and materials sampling of soils and concrete.
